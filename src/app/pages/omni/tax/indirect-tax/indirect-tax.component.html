Indirect Tax

Custom Duty/Excise Duty

To pay Direct Taxes through internet, do the following

After clicking “Proceed” button, you will be leaving PSB Digital banking and will be redirected to a third party website, which is not controlled by the Bank. Bank will not be responsible/ liable for any action, dispute etc which arises at the third party website.

  1. On the Icegate website, you will find customs e-payment gateway.
  2. Post login in the website, select the chalan you wish to pay and select Punjab and Sind Bank as payment option from list of banks and login with your PSB Digital Banking User ID and Password.
  3. You can download the receipt on successful processing of your transaction. After being redirected to Icegate, the challan for which transaction was successful will not appear in pending challan. If you still find the challan for which the payment was successful, in pending challans, please click on Verify link appearing against the challan. System will update the status and challan will disappear from the list of pending challans.

GST Payment

To pay Direct Taxes through internet, do the following

After clicking “Proceed” button, you will be leaving PSB Digital banking and will be redirected to a third party website, which is not controlled by the Bank. Bank will not be responsible/ liable for any action, dispute etc which arises at the third party website.

  1. On the Goods and Service Tax (GST) website, click on the challan Number that is applicable for your payment and enter all the relevant details.
  2. Select Punjab and Sind Bank as payment option and login with your PSB Digital Banking User ID and Password.
  3. You can download the receipt on successful processing of your transaction.
